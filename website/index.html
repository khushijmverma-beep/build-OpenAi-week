A native voice layer for macOS

Your Mac, listening when you ask.

ORISIS brings local dictation, smart writing, and a natural Realtime conversation to a quiet menu-bar app. Ask it to understand, navigate, or act—and keep control of the final step.

Built for the moments between apps

Speak naturally

Use the ORISIS hotkey for a focused, low-latency voice conversation with clear cancellation and interruption behavior.

Make words useful

Dictate locally, polish rough writing, and transform selected text without silently replacing what you selected.

Act with receipts

Open apps, navigate visible interfaces, draft Gmail, control media, and run saved workflows with explicit safety boundaries.

Install in minutes
  1. Download ORISIS and open the ZIP.
  2. Open the app; choose Allow or Open Anyway if macOS asks.
  3. Grant the microphone, Accessibility, and other requested permissions.
  4. Open Settings and add your own OpenAI API key.
  5. Use Control + Option + Q to launch ORISIS.
A personal Mac assistant

ORISIS is built for a user-owned Mac. Your key stays local, memories and workflows stay on the Mac, and consequential actions require confirmation.
